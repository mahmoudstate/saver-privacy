Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. 100% Offline (Local Storage)

Saver One is designed to work completely offline. All your accounts, budgets, and daily expenses are securely stored solely within your device's local storage, protected by your device's own security sandbox.

2. Zero Data Collection

We do not own external servers to collect your data, nor do we track your movements within the app. Your financial information is yours alone, and no third party can access it.

3. Data Safety

The app relies on your device's built-in storage to save records. Your data never leaves your device and is never transmitted over any network. Please remember that permanently clearing your app data may erase records, so we always recommend using the built-in Backup feature.

4. Device Security

Your data is protected by your device's native security features, including iOS Sandbox protection on Apple devices. We recommend keeping your device's screen lock and passcode enabled for maximum protection of your financial data.
